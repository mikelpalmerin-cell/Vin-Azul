What We Are

Not a wine club.
Not a travel company.
Something that didn't exist before.

Vin Azul Collective is the operating system for a life lived with taste. We connect the world's most curious, successful people to the winemakers, estates, and tables that most will never find — not because they lack resources, but because nobody had built the bridge.

Every experience is curated by a collective of genuine authorities: a Four Seasons hospitality veteran, an Advanced Sommelier, and a media voice that has spent years making wine culture legible to the world. This is not a platform. It is a circle of people who know what they are doing.

"When you are part of Vin Azul, you are recognized. Not famous. Recognized. The winemaker knows your palate. The hotel expects you."

We launched in California and we are building toward the world — Baja, Provence, Priorat, Tuscany, and beyond. The founding circle is fifty people. There will never be another one.
