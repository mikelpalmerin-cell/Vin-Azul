The Menu

Four Courses, Four Pairings

I  ·  First Course
Burrata & Citrus
Creamy burrata with cara cara orange segments and caramelized fennel, topped with toasted pistachios and basil oil, served with rosemary focaccia crostini
Pairing
Cellar Select Falanghina — bright, citrus-forward, mineral
II  ·  Second Course
Farfalle Spring Carbonara
Farfalle pasta, crispy pancetta, peas and lemon zest in a creamy sauce with egg yolk, browned butter, and shaved pecorino
Pairing

Braised Short Ribs
Tender slow-braised short ribs over creamy parmesan and truffle polenta, served with broccolini and charred pearl onion petals, topped with red wine demi-glace and arugula
Pairing
Sagrantino — structured, bold, extraordinary depth

Topped with mixed macerated berry compote, mascarpone cream, lime zest and candied walnuts
Pairing
Sparkling Grenache — festive, fruit-forward, celebratory
